How to Play AlphaMatrix

New to Sudoku? Master the rules in minutes and start solving like a pro.

What is Sudoku?

Sudoku is a logic-based number puzzle played on a 9×9 grid. The goal is simple: fill every cell with digits 1–9 following three strict rules — no guessing required.

It trains pattern recognition, logical deduction, and focus. No math skills needed — just pure reasoning and strategy.

AlphaMatrix elevates this timeless challenge with a futuristic interface, smart tools, and beautifully visualised puzzles.

The 3 Golden Rules

↔

One: Rows

Every row must contain the digits 1 through 9, each appearing exactly once — no repeats allowed.

↕

Two: Columns

Every column must also contain each digit from 1 to 9 exactly once — top to bottom.

⊞

Three: Boxes

Each of the nine 3×3 boxes must contain the digits 1–9 exactly once. These regions are the key constraint.

🔒

Pre-filled Clues

Some cells are pre-filled at the start. These given digits cannot be changed — they are your anchors.

✓

One Solution

Every AlphaMatrix puzzle has exactly one valid solution — no guessing needed. Logic always wins.

✦

No Math Required

The numbers are just symbols. You could use letters A–I — it's pure pattern logic, not arithmetic.

Step-by-Step Gameplay

1

Choose Your Difficulty

Select Easy, Medium, Hard, or Extreme. Harder levels remove more clues, leaving fewer anchors to work from.

Easy → Extreme
2

Tap a Cell to Select It

Click or tap any empty cell. AlphaMatrix highlights the entire row, column, and 3×3 box to help you spot conflicts instantly.

Visual Guidance
3

Enter Your Number

Tap a digit on the number pad (or press 1–9 on keyboard). Correct digits lock in; wrong ones count as a mistake — you have 3 lives.

3 Mistakes = Game Over
4

Use Notes for Possibilities

Toggle Notes Mode to pencil in candidate numbers inside a cell. Great for tracking which digits are still possible.

Press N to toggle
5

Use Hints & Undo Wisely

Stuck? Use a Hint to reveal one correct cell. Made a mistake? Undo rolls back your last move. Use them sparingly!

Limited per puzzle
✓

Complete the Board & Win!

Fill every cell correctly and AlphaMatrix celebrates your victory with a burst of confetti and your completion stats.
